Our goals

Our primary goals

At Burnley Arts Festival, our primary goals are to foster a vibrant arts community, support local artists, and make art accessible to everyone. We aim to create a platform where artists can showcase their work, connect with audiences, and inspire creativity within the community. Through our events and workshops, we strive to celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Burnley while embracing contemporary artistic practices.

Our secondary goals

In addition to our primary goals, we also focus on community engagement and education. We believe that art has the power to transform lives and bring people together, and we are committed to using art as a tool for social change. Our secondary goals include providing educational opportunities for aspiring artists, promoting diversity and inclusion within the arts community, and collaborating with local organizations to enhance the cultural landscape of Burnley. By achieving these goals, we hope to enrich the community through the arts and create a lasting impact.
