Events

Main Event

The main event will be a parade where we will showcase our talents and explore our creativity. We will have a variety of activities and performances throughout the day with many different opportunities made for people of all ages but the main event will be a parade around Burnley.

Sub Events


In addition to the main event, we will also be hosting several sub-events throughout the festival period. These include art workshops, Doodling, food stalls, and interactive installations. Each sub-event is designed to engage the community and provide a platform for local artists to showcase their work. Our interactive installations will allow visitors to experience immersive art and participate in creative activities as well as keeping all entertained. Doodling is a way for those who are feeling overwhelmed to relax and regain themselves by being given all the creative liberty they need.
