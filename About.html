About Us

Burnley Arts Festival is dedicated to promoting local artists and fostering a vibrant arts community.

Our mission is to provide a platform for artists of all disciplines to showcase their work, connect with audiences, and inspire creativity within the community. We believe that art has the power to transform lives and bring people together, and we are committed to making art accessible to everyone. Through our events, workshops, and exhibitions, we aim to celebrate the rich cultural heritage of Burnley while also embracing contemporary artistic practices. Join us in our journey to enrich the community through the arts by following us on our social media platforms.
